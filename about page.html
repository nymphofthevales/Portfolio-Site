About the Artist

Hello!

I'm a queer trans artist, programmer, and writer from Alberta, Canada. I love finding new ways to share stories and create experiences for others; through writing, games, animation, illustration... Combining these mediums and tools in new ways to create new things brings me so much joy.

This digital portfolio contains all the work that I'm proud of as well as information on the various projects I've worked on. I hope you find something of interest!

Cheers,
Athena

Process Info

For all my digital art and animation, I use Clip Studio Paint EX and a Wacom Intuos 7 tablet.

For writing and organizing larger projects, such as my Keeper of the Labyrinth game, I use Scrivener extensively, which has been an invaluable tool for me.

For my geometric tiled drawings and animations, I tend to plan the figures first on paper with compass and straightedge.
